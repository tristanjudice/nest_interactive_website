INTERGALACTIC PUZZLE TOUCHDOWN

Intergalactic Puzzle Touchdown is a turn-based PvP puzzle game inspired by classic Match-3 games infused with the mechanics of gridiron football and a comedic sci-fi setting.

Swap tiles to make matches and earn YARDS to advance down the field. Place BLOCKERS in your opponent's path to stop their drive. Combine BIG PLAYS for powerful effects, use TEAM POWERS to warp the rules, and push your luck while avoiding PENALTIES.
